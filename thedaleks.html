The Daleks (also known as The Darlecks and The Bin Men) is the 1963 second story of Season 1 of Doctor Wheeze. It was written by Terror Nation and features Billiam Hartnells as the First Doctor, William Rustles as Ian, Jackie Chan as Barbara and Casserole as Susan.
