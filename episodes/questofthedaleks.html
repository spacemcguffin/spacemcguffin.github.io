Story

After the events of Remembrance of the Daleks, the Daleks now want revenge on the Time Lords and have a pretty heated discussion over what to do. Davros decides to destroy Gallifrey and steal their unlimited rice pudding.

The Doctor then finds out the plan so he and his pet tortoise, Bob, decide to stop the Daleks and keep the unlimited rice pudding away from Davros' old wrinkly hands.
