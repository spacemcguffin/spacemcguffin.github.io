Trivia

  • The EP had the working titles Reel Deep (Covers) and Deep Blue Theatre. Lewis admitted the first of those titles would fit the EP better, but he preferred Deep Blue Cinema so it stuck.
  • Before the EP became a compilation of cover parodies, the original song Shelley Got Crabs was originally intended to feature.
