Outfit

The Pompous Doctor is usually dressed in a boating blazer consisting of multiple colours, a white shirt with question marks on each side of the collar, a waistcoat & tie, and trousers that match the colours of his jacket or waistcoat.

His first known outfit consisted of a blue & red jacket with white stripes, a yellow tie, either a brown or yellow patterned waistcoat, and blue checked trousers. In Convolution of the Doctor, the Pompous Doctor acquires a gold pug brooch from future companion Christie Christwald.

Following an encounter with a future version of his current incarnation, the Pompous Doctor changes his outfit as part of a plan to avoid the future presented to him. This "darker" outfit includes a red jacket with black stripes, a black shirt with no question marks, a black tie, and a red waistcoat. He would wear this outfit during his travels with Tim and Wren Stark, during his war against his other incarnations, and even after he sees the error of his ways. Eventually, in The Greatest of Friends, the Pompous Doctor recovers his previous outfit from a charity shop and wears it during an investigation into a painting of himself and The Sorry Doctor.

In The Pompous Redemption, having spent a thousand years in a self-imposed exile, the Pompous Doctor's "toothpaste" outfit has become dishevelled and he has grown a large scruffy beard. After being recovered by Tim, now Lord President, the Pompous Doctor's beard is shaved off and he is presented with a new outfit designed by The Scientist to wear during his mission to stop The Narrator. He would continue wearing this outfit after he resumes his "aimless wanderings" alongside new companions Mute and The Passenger.

His current outfit includes a primarily purple jacket with stripes in a multitude of different colours (including shades of blue and red), a white shirt with a red trim & red buttons on each side of the collar (replacing the question marks), yellow tie, a light yellow cloth waistcoat, yellow trousers, and the return of the pug brooch.

Adventures

Early Life

The Doctor wakes up to find the TARDIS shrinking and closing in on him. Dazed and confused, he must find the correct switch in order to reset the dimensional stabiliser, or be crushed to death. (Smaller on the Inside)

The Doctor receives a distress signal from the planet Skaro. Upon arrival, the Daleks tell him they sent no such signal, as their planet is peaceful and thriving. The Daleks are peaceful now. The Doctor locates Davros, who reveals he was the one who sent the distress signal. The Doctor tells Davros that the Daleks being a force for good will help heal the universe and allow millions of beings to live in peace. The Doctor leaves. Davros gets really mad and decides the only option is to blow up Skaro and destroy the nice Daleks. (Generosity of the Daleks)

The Doctor sets course for the leisure planet, Equilibrium, for a holiday. On the way, the TARDIS is held up by space traffic caused by the Meddling Space Warden. (The Space Jam)
