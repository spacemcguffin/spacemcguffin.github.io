About

The Eye of Orion is a planet known to be one of the most tranquil places in the universe. The Diamond Doctor listed it as his third favorite planet. (A Change of Hearts)

Disheartened, the Doctor looks for a new way to extend his identity's brand and turns to Jack and Bonham, companions of the Renegade Doctor- who both suggest a podcast. The Doctor returns to the TARDIS with a successful podcast under his Tiny Hat, and presents his mission statement to go out into the universe, informing everyone of his new image.
