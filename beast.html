Story

The Doctor attempts to read 50 Shades of Grey, while Ninja Bob flies the TARDIS to Paris, looking for a peaceful holiday where no aliens invasions are taking place.

Having arrived there, The Doctor gets increasingly frustrated at the lack of alien invasions- only for The Daleks to arrive. However, nobody is panicking due to the amount of alien invasions that go on in their time.

The Doctor goes onto the Dalek ship to stop them, only to be captured, laving it up to Ninja Bob to save the day- which he does by finding the Scroll of Ferrojitzu, which he uses to turn the Eiffel Tower into a living weapon.

After restoring the tower, Ninja Bob tells The Doctor that he's leaving him, and he wants his normal life back, with SARA and the ability to mourn the loss of his dead brother. He then departs, leaving The Doctor shocked silent.
