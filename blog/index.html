12.04.23 — smiling through tears

10:15pm. The sky is so clear tonight. There are so many stars. I’ve not done this in a while; just sat, calm, taking in the world, enjoying the quiet. Sometimes it’s dangerous ‘cos it leads to introspection and overthinking, but I love the marvel of the universe. It’s funny how the stars, which are millions of miles away, keep me grounded. I find them so beautiful and wonderful. I feel lucky that I get to witness them. It’s always sad when they’re hidden by clouds.

The world is quiet. Everything seems peaceful. I’m smiling through tears.

11.02.22

2:30am. The night sky is so clear tonight. Snow is dusting the tops of houses around me. So pretty. Absolute silence, and peace. Momentary bliss. Being a night owl isn’t always a bad thing.

We all get so wrapped up in politics and work and technology and anxiety and other people that we forget just how beautiful this planet and this universe is.

There’s something quite fairytale about snow-dusted rooftops against a starry sky. It’s nice to just let go and feel like a kid again. A world full of wonder and magic. I should enjoy the night sky more often. It does wonders for the soul.
