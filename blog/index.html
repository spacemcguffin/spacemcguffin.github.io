12.04.23 — smiling through tears

10:15pm. The sky is so clear tonight. There are so many stars. I’ve not done this in a while; just sat, calm, taking in the world, enjoying the quiet. Sometimes it’s dangerous ‘cos it leads to introspection and overthinking, but I love the marvel of the universe. It’s funny how the stars, which are millions of miles away, keep me grounded. I find them so beautiful and wonderful. I feel lucky that I get to witness them. It’s always sad when they’re hidden by clouds.

The world is quiet. Everything seems peaceful. I’m smiling through tears.
