Personal Timeline

At the Academy

The Master went to the Time Lord Academy with the Original Doctor. They were best friends until he started turning evil (stealing the Doctor's socks and burning them). Ultimately, it was a prank played on the Doctor by the Master and Borusa that resulted in the end of his friendship with the Doctor.

The Master Dalek Plan

In order to pay for his numerous evil schemes, the Master begins working part-time on weekends at Space Tesco. He worked on the checkout counter until an encounter with the Pompous Doctor. He was subsequently fired for overcharging the Doctor five credits.

Appearance

The Master dresses in a classic nefarious black suit, with a black tie and gloves. Sometimes he has a beard.

List of Appearances

The Master's TARDIS
Type TARDIS
Place of origin Gallifrey

Like the Doctor before him, an early incarnation of the Master stole his TARDIS when he decided to leave Gallifrey. It is thought that the Master was inspired by the Doctor to steal a TARDIS and flee his home planet. The Master stole a Type 45, a model comparatively more advanced than the Doctor's.
