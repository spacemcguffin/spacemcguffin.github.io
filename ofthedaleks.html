    • Notes

  • This story developed from the idea of referencing as many Dalek stories as possible. This joke very quickly got out of hand, and the production team began referencing as many events 'of the Daleks' as they could, including: Promise of the Daleks, Prophecy of the Daleks, Destiny of the Daleks, Queen of the Daleks, Regeneration of the Daleks, Pay Rise of the Daleks, Genesis of the Daleks, Catalogue of the Daleks, Master Plan of the Daleks, Annual Contract of the Daleks, Restaurant Chain of the Daleks and Sisterhood of the Daleks.
