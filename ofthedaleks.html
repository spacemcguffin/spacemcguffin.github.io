The Monster Mash is the first episode of the Tiny Hat Doctor's series of adventures.

Story

The Tiny Hat Doctor arrives in a spooky mansion, wearing a specially themed tiny hat. He encounters Dracula and after discussing hit film series Hotel Transylvania, The Doctor establishes his catchphrase as "FINE."

The Doctor discovers that there is a Spirit haunting Dracula's Mansion and decides to stop it - heading to the party room. The Doctor does a speech to reason with the Spirit, who agrees to become the DJ for Dracula's party. They all play the song from the end of the first Hotel Transylvania film as the Doctor leaves.

    • Notes

  • This story developed from the idea of referencing as many Dalek stories as possible. This joke very quickly got out of hand, and the production team began referencing as many events 'of the Daleks' as they could, including: the Promise of the Daleks, the Prophecy of the Daleks, the Destiny of the Daleks, the Queen of the Daleks, the Regeneration of the Daleks, the Pay Rise of the Daleks, the Genesis of the Daleks, the Renaissance of the Daleks, the Voicemail/span> of the Daleks, the Catalogue of the Daleks, the Master Plan of the Daleks, the Space War of the Daleks, the Annual Contract of the Daleks, the Restaurant Chain of the Daleks and the Sisterhood of the Daleks.
