The Crap Out is the fifth episode of Season 28, and the 338th overall episode of South Park. It aired on December 10, 2025.

Synopsis

Satan's due, Stan's praying, and only a Christmas miracle can deliver the Antichrist on time.

Plot

"We need some goddamn Christmas magic!" ― Stan

At South Park Elementary, Stan is seeing the school counsellor [1] and talking about how tough the last few months have been, mentioning ICE raids, his dad's company going out of business, and losing their home and moving in with his grandfather at Shady Acres and admits he isn't able to cope with all of the change around them.
