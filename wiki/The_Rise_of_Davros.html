The Rise of Davros is a minisode that precedes the first episode of the Fractal Doctor's first wave, Strange Matters.

Story

The Doctor stumbles into the genesis of the Daleks and causes a wibble in the timeline. As a result, Davros rises and things will never be the same again™.
