The Floppy Disk Ate My Homework.

The Floppy Disk Ate My Homework
Artist Lewis Christian
Released 2005

The Floppy Disk Ate My Homework is the first EP (extended play) by Lewis Christian. It was initially distributed via a limited run of 100 floppy disks. It was then released in 2005 on Lewis' MySpace page, but has since been removed. The EP contains three tracks.

Track Listing

  1. Aware of Computers
  2. 49/50 (and Still Not Over It)
  3. Shutting Down

Trivia

  • The title of this EP is sometimes abbreviated to Floppy Ate My Homework or simply Floppy.
  • The inspiration for Floppy came about when Lewis started high school and some of his friends were pursuing music and creating bands. The three tracks are based around incidents from Lewis' time at primary school.
