The Rise of Davros is a minisode that precedes the first episode of the Fractal Doctor's first wave, Strange Matters.

Story

The Doctor stumbles into the genesis of the Daleks and causes a wibble in the timeline. As a result, Davros rises and things will never be the same again™.

Cast

  • The Doctor - Kris Marshall
  • Davros - Noel Edmonds
  • Snyder - Hector Bumblebridge

    • Uncredited

  • Gharman - Matthew Waterhouse

    • Notes

  • The Rise of Davros was inspired by the official 2023 Children in Need minisode Destination: Skaro and comments expressed by Russell T Davies regarding the visual changes made to the character of Davros for 2023 and beyond.
  • This story sees Davros able to rise from his chariot and walk free from his life support machine, thanks to the interference of the Fractal Doctor. His left arm, however, remains in suspiciously tucked into his pocket at all times. Whilst deciding on appropriate trousers to wear, Davros tries on denim jeans, skin-tight leather trousers, and a kilt.
  • A working title for this minisode was Regenesis of the Daleks.
