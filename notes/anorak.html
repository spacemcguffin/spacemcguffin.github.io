My name is Lewis and I’m a Doctor Who fan.

I started watching the show in 2006. I bought the entire series on DVD, and then I bought the entire series again when it was re-released on blu-ray. I’ve seen several stories multiple times, and it’s probably unhealthy.

My favourite Doctors are William Hartnell, Sylvester McCoy and, because I’m a basic bitch, David Tennant. I’m also very fond of the second David Tennant. Don’t ask – it’s complicated.

I also prefer Cybermen over Daleks.
