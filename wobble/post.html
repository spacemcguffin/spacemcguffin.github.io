Big Finish License Extended To 2035

A companion article template that matches your podcasts page style — hero tile on the right, and a full article panel below.

Read Back to podcasts
News • 20 February 2026 • — min read

Big Finish’s audio output has become one of the most reliable parts of the expanded universe. With the license extended until the end of 2035, the team can plan longer arcs and invest in more ambitious ranges.

What the extension changes

The biggest benefit is stability: multi-year story planning, easier scheduling for returning talent, and a clearer runway for spin-offs and experimental formats.

“This gives us room to plan ahead and keep telling stories fans love — and stories that surprise them.”

What to expect next

Watch for announcements across the next few months: new box sets, returning favourites, and (likely) bigger cross-range events.
