License Extended To End Of 2035

Audio drama specialists announce a long-term extension—meaning more stories for years to come.

Publish info
Words by Garry • 8 February 2026
Reading time
2 mins
Tags
Big Finish Audio Doctor Who

At times, it feels like the audio universe will be around forever — and this news makes it official: stories are locked in for a long run.

This extension means guaranteed adventures until the end of 2035 — plenty of time for new ranges, returning favourites, and bigger swings.

Big Finish has played a pivotal role in expanding the universe… delivering stories that honour the legacy and bring fans new adventures.

Why it matters

Long renewals help planning: longer arcs, more ambitious series, and consistent releases you can actually look forward to.

What’s next

Expect more announcements as the pipeline opens up. In the meantime, check the back catalogue, grab a jumping-on point, and enjoy the ride.
