Shitty little chocolates

I hate advent calendars. Last year, I wasn't allowed one because, the year before that, I'd scoffed a whole calendar in one go. This year, I've been treated to one and it's going better. I've eaten day 1, day 2 and day 18 because 18 was a chunky chocolate. Yep, I cut out the plastic bit to check. It's only Dairy Milk too, so it's nothing special. But yay for extra chocolate. It's now officially December and we're on the final countdown to Christmas.

Whatthefuck. Above where I write and edit entries, it shows a posting date and time. I started this entry at 3 minutes to midnight on December 1st, and it's suddenly started updating itself. It never used to do that. Grrr, Livejournal, you sneaky cunts. Fuck that though. I'm gunna edit it back to when I started this entry. Bollocks to your system!

Anyway.

Today was rather nice. I had Paul for literature instead of Donna (^_^), chill time in media (^_^), chill time in language (^_^), doodle time in general studies (^_^) and fun times in ICT (^_^).

General studies has now officially become my doodle lesson. Last week, I drew random things such as a Chocolate Orange Church and Tetris Towers, whereas this week I drew a little Christmas Village. In addition to that, ICT has now become a lot better. Why? Coz Terry's off for a few weeks, apparently. I still have work to do, and deadlines, but it'll be so much more relaxed and peaceful without him. And then, to round off my happiness, I'm not going to college tomorrow - literature is cancelled and there's no point just going in for a ten-minute tutorial. Great stuff!

I spoke to Charlie earlier too, for the first time in about three weeks.

SHE'S STILL ALIVE WOO!

It snowed a little earlier. Not much. Nothing fantastic. But it still snowed. Looking at last December's entries, I notice that 2nd December was a snow day. Part of me hopes that happens but part of me wants it to wait til Thursday when I'm meant to be in college.

That be all :)
