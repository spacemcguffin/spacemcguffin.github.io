Making music

Currently listening to Farewell 42, an AI-generated song. I saw someone post an AI-generated song on Twitter the other day and I had to give it a go. Ended up buying a load of credits, so I’ve had a blast generating a few different songs.

You can input a prompt and it’ll make an entirely new song from scratch, complete with vocals, in a genre of your choice. You can also input your own lyrics. I decided to generate a punk rock song about Charlotte’s old school-era band, The Farewell 42s.

Obviously, the AI technology is still quite early (the vocals are very auto-tuney) but it’s quite mind-blowing that I can generate a competent (almost passable) song within literally minutes. The app also allows you to remix, add more to the song, etc. It’s addicting. I’m going through a phase, much like I did last year when I discovered an app for generating AI artwork based on your own prompts. This app also auto-generates a thumbnail image too, based on the lyrics.
