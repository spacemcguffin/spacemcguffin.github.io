Shitty little illness ¬¬

Day off today. Yay and rawrrrr.

Yay because, well, yay. Was meant to work tonight but Nicola swapped shifts with me so I’m working Friday night now instead. And mum and Steve have been at work all day til 6pm. Rawrrr because I’m ill (thanks, Luke, for passing it on!) with a headache, a sore throat and a non-stop cold. I dunno whether this is a "yay" or a "rawr"... I didn’t do any ICT work today. That was my plan. To get some done. But I haven’t had the energy. All I’ve done today is looked after Millie, browsed the internet, watched TV and wrapped a few presents. But I’ve heard that Terry’s off til after Christmas, so deadlines can be bent and re-scheduled - oh yes!

I’m gunna go to bed now and hope I feel better in the morning. The ideal tomorrow would be a snow day in which I feel 100% better and can go sledging with Kelly. But I doubt that’ll happen (watch it happen now xD). Seriously though. If I feel like I do now, I’m gunna give college a miss if mum lets me. I mean, Luke’s had two days off school (he’ll probably get tomorrow off too) so it’s likely I may get time off if I’m ill too. Best do anyway. But yeah. We shall see.

Goodnight world.
