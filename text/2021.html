19 January

I’m awake at 3am again. I feel grim and horrible. I know it’s not the answer or the solution to everything but I wish I had someone like C****. I wish I had someone to love, and someone who loved me. I just feel trapped and alone, and stuck. I should sleep.

16 January

Covid has set me right back, just as I’d gained a new sense of optimism and drive. Lockdown has re-isolated me, pushed me back into a shell, trapped me in four walls for days and weeks at a time. Constantly wavering between having an identity crisis, and just distracting myself completely to avoid the world and any sense of responsibility.
