new year’s eve

The house is so quiet without Millie either snoring away or poking her head around the door whenever we’re in the kitchen. It’s the first time in my life that I’ve not had an animal in the house – it’s very strange.

millie

We had to put Millie to sleep earlier. She’s not been well for a while now, and she’s just deteriorated over the last couple of days. Struggling to breathe, no energy, sleeping the days away. It was the kindest thing, and tonight when I got in from work she just completely collapsed and couldn’t walk at all. Hopefully she’s curled up with Tia and Ellie and the rest of the gang now.

Love you Mills x
