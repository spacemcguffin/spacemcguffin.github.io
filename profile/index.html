ABOUT

i’m lewis. i’m in my early 30s and have been using the internet for over 20 years now. although, in the past decade, i’ve become a social media hermit. the internet i once loved has been lost to time, like many of my former usernames. i’m enjoying the peace and quiet now, with my own space here. my own space. my space. nice.

After two decades frequenting the likes of Piczo, Bebo, Blogger, MySpace, Dailybooth, Tumblr, LiveJournal, Wordpress, Facebook, Twitter, and other forums, I can’t keep up anymore. I’m exhausted. I’m also old, and just way more private than I used to be. Between 2008 and 2012, I wrote an online diary on LiveJournal. For years later, I posted over 25,000 tweets. Then, one random day in the mid-2010s, I just nuked it all. Gone, at the click of a button. Now, I just have a couple of empty profiles around the internet, purely for browsing and lurking from the shadows, and for private messaging friends.

And I have this website. I’ve mostly used it for playing around with CSS and HTML, creating templates and just fucking about with code. Most of my stuff has been temporary. The only fixed feature of lewis.lc is an archive of my diary entries. Using the Wayback Machine, I’ve been able to salvage most titles from 2008 and 2009, though the rest are gone. Since 2021, I’ve resumed writing a diary but it’s offline.
