The Master

BC
Incarnations Frivolous, Beige, Pompous
Species Time Lord
Place of origin Gallifrey

The Master is a rogue Time Lord that is occasionally described as the Doctor's "best enemy". He's also very evil.

The Master's TARDIS is stuck as a red telephone box.
