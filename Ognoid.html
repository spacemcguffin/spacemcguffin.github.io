The Ognoids were a humanoid species neighboring Planetary Union space. They were notable for their protracted conflict with the Ominox over control of the planet Equilibrium.

Biology

Ognoids were about the same height as adult humans, entirely hairless, with long, bony horns encompassing their heads. They lacked eyebrows or lashes. Navarian skin was light purple, rough, and coarse.

Culture

The Ognoids were "slender, reserved, and a little sarcastic," but also subtle and intelligent. They spoke a language that was similar to the Bruidians, which reflected their common origin.

Their hatred of the Bruidians was infamous, and they could be at once both terrible and menacing to the Bruidians while gracious and kind to other species.

At least one Ognoid vessel was identified, the Navarian cruiser. A second vessel was a Ognoid warship used in battle with the Bruidians.

They enjoyed meals of Cavellian spiceroot.

History

Around 27,581 BCE, a technologically primitive species lived on Lopovius. Over time, the species evolved into both the Ognoids and the Bruidians.

Thousands of years later, both species had forgotten their common ancestry and laid claim to the planet. The dispute led to an intense military conflict for control.

In mid-2419, archaeologists discovered an ancient birthing bucket recovered from the planet's original inhabitants. If genetic material could be found on the bucket and tested, Bruidian and Ognoid governments agreed that a genetic match with the modern species would establish rightful domain over Lopovius.
