Death to the Davros

Death to the Davros is the fourth episode of the Pompous Doctor's second wave, Walkies in the Death Zone.

Summary

Davros is dying, and his final visitor also happens to be his mortal enemy.

Story

The Doctor turns to up to visit Davros in hospital - who is dying of old age - so that he can primarily gloat. When Martin convinces The Doctor to keep visiting, however, they become good friends and bond as "BFF"s. However, after a game of wheelchair jousting, Davros finally starts to truly die. After Davros has died, the Daleks command the Doctor to mourn him, and he obeys.
