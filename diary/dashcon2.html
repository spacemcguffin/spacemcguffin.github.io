The Pompous Doctor is a groundbreaking portrayal of the character, which re-invents him in a new and exciting way! See the Doctor as you've never seen him before.

Pompous travels the universe, usually alone because, after all, he is the lonely god. He likes to brood and think about the Time War a lot, however this doesn't stop him from being cheeky and fun times!

Story

With their new mission to unravel the Voord Conspiracy, the Doctor, Meglos Jr. and Ko Sharmus arrive at a significant manipulation in the timeline - Illinois, 2014. Upon landing, the team encounter a large convention hall, boasting a single ball pit at its heart, and the Doctor realises the true nature of their location, for this is Dashcon! Due to both Meglos Jr. and Ko Sharmus being out of their own time, the Doctor explains Dashcon - an absolutely shit convention held for Tumblr that changed internet culture forever with it being so bad.

Exploring the building further, the gang locate the infamous ball pit, but find the attendees are embracing it and even enjoying their time there. The Doctor immediately suspects something is amiss and continues his investigation alone, leaving his two companions at the ball pit. With his Tiny Hat set to scanning mode, he is drawn to an inconspicuous vending machine, but after opening it up, the Doctor stumbles into a dark reflection of his own TARDIS control room - the Master's TARDIS!

The Master reveals his next step in the Voord Conspiracy is to make Dashcon a success, leaving the Voord to reap the rewards once they take over. Using his secret spherical hypnotising devices, the Master has brainwashed all attendees into believing the convention is amazing, and cackles that the Doctor will never be able to guess where he's hidden them. The Doctor immediately guesses correctly that they're in the ball pit, much to the Master's annoyance, before dispelling of his opponent with a swift chop of Venusian Aikido.

Rushing back to the ball pit, the Doctor finds his two companions relishing and frolicking in the play area, they too having fallen under the Master's hideous spell. With time running out, the Doctor resorts to drastic measures in order to preserve history and ruin Dashcon forever - he must piss into the ball pit! As chaos descends, the Doctor pulls Meglos Jr. and Ko Sharmus back to the TARDIS, furious with himself for having to let Dashcon perish for the sake of the future. Ko appeals to his better nature, pleading with him to just save someone- and to the Doctor's surprise, he does just that. With their work in Illinois now finished, the Doctor senses there is something even bigger at play than just the Voord Conspiracy, and that all they have now is the Master's breadcrumbs to follow...

...but before they can do anything, they all need a shower as they absolutely stink of piss.

Notes

  • As with Act I, Act II was written in a single continuous session - this time on 15 Jan 2021.
  • The idea of a pure historical set at Dashcon always amused Jacob, so he finally wrote it into this 'anything can happen' series.
  • Originally there was going to be a fourth episode in Act II, but Jacob had no idea to riff sufficiently on (barring the finale arc starting in The Picasso Trap) and felt the energy of the writing process wind down after Fires of Dashcon, so he left it there as to not let the series lose momentum.
