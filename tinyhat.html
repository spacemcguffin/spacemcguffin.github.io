Story

Contemplating his new identity, the Tiny Hat Doctor arrives in modern day London with his companions Tsukasa, Daiki, and Dolmio Man. They soon encounter a deadly invasion force of Cybermen, which are quickly destroyed by Tsukasa and Daiki due to their superhero status. The Doctor attempts to have a showdown with the Cyber-Leader, but is shown up by Dolmio Man destroying him by presenting an existential query: whens'a your Dolmio day?

Disheartened, the Doctor looks for a new way to extend his identity's brand and turns to Jack and Bonham, companions of the Renegade Doctor- who both suggest a podcast. The Doctor returns to the TARDIS with a successful podcast under his Tiny Hat, and presents his mission statement to go out into the universe, informing everyone of his new image.

  • The Tiny Hat doctor originates from a joke tweet Jacob and Phoenix made on the PZP twitter in January 2020. Naturally, Jacob later took this too far and made actual content with this character.
  • This episode was produced incredibly quickly, with it originating from a joke selfie of Phoenix wearing the Halloween tiny hat sent on Monday 26th, Jacob writing the script very early on the 27th, and the line recording and editing all being done on the 30th.
