remnants

may 16th, 2021

Fell down a Twitter rabbit hole, searching my old usernames. Obviously, all my tweets are gone forever, but there are so many replies still floating around and bizarre bits of media attached to them too. Managed to find a couple of old photos of myself – bizarre to think they’re probably just going to languish in the depths of early 2010s Twitter forever, unless those who posted them ever delete their accounts. Also served as a reminder of how long I’ve been on the fucking thing, and how old some of my friends’ accounts are.
