About

Not much is known about this incarnation of the Master, but he's probably really generic and likes wearing black. He's also young because this is the first Master.

Adventures

Birth

He was born on Gallifrey, and was an only child.

Growing Up

As the first Master, this incarnation grew up on Gallifrey along with the Original Doctor and Young Rani. The Master attended the Time Lord Academy, but evilly skived most of his classes.

Later, the Doctor slept with the Master's girlfriend.

The Master and the Doctor's friendship inevitably turned to shit and the Master went off to pursue universal domination, like you do.
