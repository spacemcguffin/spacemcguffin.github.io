Character

The President is a stern, unfair, racist leader of America. He was a big fan of war, until the Doctor's speech motivated him to take a more peaceful route, but this only lasted a week before he got the nuclears back out. He can be found looking over America when flying on Eagle One, or cowering in the Oval Office.
