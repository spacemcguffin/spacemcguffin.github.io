About

Regeneration is a biological process imprinted in the DNA of a Time Lord. Described as a "miracle," regeneration allows a Time Lord to survive fatal injuries. At the moment of mortality, every cell in their body is renewed- albeit, with the caveat of their current appearance and personality changing into a new form.

Whilst their memories and inherent beliefs are usually retained between each incarnation, the faces and tastes often differ after every regeneration. However, bodies have been known to be repeated across regeneration cycles, with only slight physical or vocal alterations to separate them from their initial emergence. It is also not uncommon for the individual's clothes to also regenerate, but this only occurs in rare circumstances and is usually unnoticeable to most.

Typically, regeneration is a permanent process that cannot be undone naturally- but this does not mean it hasn't happened. Degeneration can be forced onto a Time Lord by a variety of means, including weapons designed by other Time Lords and highly-intelligent races. The results of these reversals can have disastrous consequences for the recipient's mental stability as personalities are regressed, possibly leading to future incarnations suffering long-term psychological damage beyond post-regenerative trauma.

Other species have been known to extract and harness regeneration from Time Lords to use for themselves. However, permanent regeneration cycles are usually impossible to gain as Time Lord blood inherently cannot mix with non-Gallifreyan genes, corrupting the regenerative cells.
