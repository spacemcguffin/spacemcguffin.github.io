escapism. 01/02/23

It’s been a week since I last put pen to paper, fingers to keys. I went back to work on Sunday and it was okay, it was nice. And it’s ricocheted downhill since then. I almost broke down today, but held myself together just enough to get through the day. I don’t want to talk about stuff, so I’m not going to. Maybe I will in another entry, but for now I just want to space out a little and lose myself in some escapism.

That escapism is music, as it has been for the whole of January.
