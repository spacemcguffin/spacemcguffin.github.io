Cybermen

Cybermen
Other Names Cybertrons, Human 2.0
Home Planet Mondas, Telos, Earth, Planet 14, Bonk
First Appearance Cybermania

The Cybermen are a bunch of nasty bully-face cyborgs, devoid of emotion, who convert other humans to their cause. Through their multiple origins, the Cybermen have been involved in many conflicts and taken many forms, evolving as they lose continuously. They also go into hibernation/hiding a lot, and return every thousand years or so with a new design.
