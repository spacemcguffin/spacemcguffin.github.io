The Cybermen are a bunch of nasty bully-face cyborgs who are totes emotionless n' shit, who convert other humans to their cause. Through their multiple origins, the Cybermen have been involved in many conflicts and taken many forms, evolving as they lose continuously. They also go into hibernation/hiding a lot, and return every thousand years or so with a new design.

History

Origins

As a young boy, Davros was visited by Santa Claus, who hesitated to give Davros his LEGO set due to the evil acts Davros would later commit. However, Santa eventually decided to give Davros his present, both because he believed in redemption and also the Christmas Goblins would takeover if he did not. (The Day of the Davros)

The Age of Tinfoil

Davros later became the Evil Creator of the Daleks. (Skaro’s Lament)
