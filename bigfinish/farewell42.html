“Welcome back, Doctor...”

Centuries ago on the war-torn planet Skaro, a great scientist created the most dangerous creatures the universe would ever know: the Daleks. At their genesis, Davros first met—and was defeated by—the Doctor.

Across the years and throughout space they fought, a conflict that appeared to end with the destruction of Skaro. Except Davros survived. Alone in the dark, thoughts of revenge kept him alive. Now the Doctor is back, Davros is waiting, and their destiny is at hand.
