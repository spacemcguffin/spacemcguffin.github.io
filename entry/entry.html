End of the Road.

Part 1 • Old Man Yells at Cloud

I stayed up ‘til about 4am watching the first Presidential debate between Kamala Harris and the fat old orange man. Harris wiped the floor with him. She set so many traps, he took the bait every time, and he ended up just ranting and looking like a fucking madman. (How this election is even close is beyond me, but dumb Americans I guess.) It was a good night for her and I hope sanity prevails in November.

Part 2 • Journey’s End

I just finished watching the final episode of The Grand Tour. God, it was good. I never kept up with Grand Tour (maybe one day I’ll watch their specials) but I’ve always dipped in and out of Clarkson, Hammond and May’s projects. I loved their Top Gear specials, found myself hooked on Top Gear for a short period, and I’ve watched plenty of clips online of their stuff since the move from BBC to Amazon.

One for the Road was a wonderful two-hour special to round off their journey together. Wistful, beautiful, silly, epic. It’s a shame their journey together has come to an end, but what a fitting end. After the chaos of how they ended Top Gear, it was so good to see them go out in style and on their own terms here. Bloody great. They’ll be missed.
