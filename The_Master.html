About

The Demon Headmaster is a megalomaniac incarnation of the Master who seeks to rule the entire world, thinking society would be better off under his control - which would make the world ordered but ultimately little more than a police-state without free thought or will.

He is tall and thin, dressed in an immaculate black suit. From his shoulders, a long black teacher's gown hangs in heavy folds, like wings, giving him the appearance of a huge crow. Only his head is startlingly white. Fair hair, almost as colourless as snow, lay around a face with almost paper white skin and pallid lips. His eyes are hidden behind dark glases, like two black holes in the middle of all the whiteness. When it comes to children he shows no mercy whatsoever.


The Original Master

The Original Master

First appearance Time of Tom

"Damn and blast! Oh, heck!"

Not much is known about this incarnation of the Master, but he's probably really generic and likes wearing black. He's also young because he's the first Master.

Original Adventures

He was born on Gallifrey, and was an only child.

As the first Master, this incarnation grew up on Gallifrey along with the Original Doctor and Young Rani. The Master attended the Time Lord Academy, but evilly skived most of his classes. At a young age, the Master looked into the Untempered Schism and went mad. In a blind fit of rage, he pushed Borusa into it and claimed his first victim. (Time of Tom)

Later, the Doctor slept with the Master's girlfriend.

The Master and the Doctor's friendship inevitably turned to shit and the Master went off to pursue universal domination, like you do. (Master of the Universe)

    The Definitive Master
    Other Names The Master
    Species Time Lord
    First appearance Catalogue of Monsters

    "You'll get no help from me, Silurian!"
    ― The Definitive Master

    This is the Definitive Master, the one who's done most of the bad stuff. He sometimes refers to himself as 'the Definite Master' because he's also dyslexic. We don't know what his outfit looks like because he wears a massive black cape over it. He also carries an evil cane which holds his sonic cigars.

    Master Plans

    Mysterious Origins

    It is unknown how this incarnation came to be, and what caused his previous self to regenerate. This is because there are rumours of a Problematic Master incarnation, believed to have been wiped from existence by the Definitive Master. Nobody knows why the Master would erase one of his previous selves, especially since he revels in chaos and badness, but he did so it's canon, accept it.

    Catalogue of Evil Schemes

    The Definitive Master teamed up with a whole array of monsters, including Daleks, Cybermen, Zuckernoids and the Rani. Most of his nefarious schemes were undone by the Doctor, or the Master noped out at the last minute because he couldn't trust anyone else. (Catalogue of Monsters)

    Travelling alone, he spent years cooking up his next dastardly plot. In the meantime, he painted his TARDIS console room black with gold trimmings and adopted a robot parrot. (Darkness of Doom)

    He once left an exploding message in a bottle in space for the Doctor to find, but a future incarnation who forgot about it found it instead and regenerated after being blown up. (Dear Doctor)

    Fall of the Master

    The Master crashes into a school and all the kids start nicking his pens, so he can't write any more evil ideas down. He vows revenge and kills the headteacher to take his place. Unfortunately he trips on a Sharpie, falls down the stairs and regenerates. (The Master's Bad Day)
