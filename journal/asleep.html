10.02.21 — the world’s asleep

2:30am. The night sky is so clear tonight. So many stars just littering the sky. So pretty.

We all get so wrapped up in politics and work and technology and anxiety and other people that we forget just how beautiful this planet and this universe is.

Poked my head out of the window. Felt the winter breeze on my chest. The stars gently flickering in the sky. The world asleep, so quiet, so calm.
