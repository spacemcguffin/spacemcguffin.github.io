falling out of love

I feel somewhat lost and empty. All the things I once loved are either going sour, or I’m falling out of love with them, or they just don’t exist anymore. Friendships are fleeting; communication amongst friends is dead in general; adult life is just grey. If nothing else, I’ll welcome lighter evenings and the arrival of colour back into the world; that should keep me afloat at least. I wish I felt more fulfilled, but I don’t know how to be or what to do.

I’m just going through the motions.

I just want to be in someone’s arms, being looked after, being reassured, being loved. Feels like it’s never gunna happen. My lust and passion and spirit is just gone. I feel vacant. Maybe I put off going to bed until silly hours of the morning because I want to put off tomorrow for as long as possible, and the only time I feel a semblance of normality is late at night when the world is peaceful.
