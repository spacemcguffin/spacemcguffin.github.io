22.08.21 — fading memories

There are so many years of my life now that I don’t have any record of.

I remember milestones and certain moments but the past is becoming hazier and hazier in my mind, and I feel like I’ve lost a grasp on so much of who I used to be; my childhood, and anything of note beyond the last decade (which, itself, has largely been a blur).

I’ve got a handful of photos to look back on, but all my old tweets are gone, my old diary is lost to time, and my memory is slowly fading to white. And in some respects, that’s okay, that’s good, you have to keep looking forwards and moving on, but I also feel some sadness that I struggle to remember how I got here, what I used to feel, what I used to like, who I used to be.
