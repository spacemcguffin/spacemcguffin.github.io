spin around the sun

monday, january 1st, 2024
00:14am

During the London fireworks there was a shot of a crowd of people, most of whom were holding up their phones and recording the display. Don’t these people know it’s being televised and streamed online for them to watch in HD, from all angles, literally minutes after it’s finished? You queue all day, pay however much for a ticket and travel, all to watch the fireworks through your phone? And then you end up with a crappy video on your phone for your Instagram story that you’ll never watch again. Talk about living in the moment. Sigh.

All I want from 2024 is a little bit of emotional stability, and a calmer year in general.

My iced berry lemonade candle is burning and flickering away, and rain is lashing against my window. Everyone else is asleep. Another quiet new year for me. I don’t feel emotional about it this time. Maybe I’ll get all wistful and melancholy later. Right now, I’m just calm – breathing, typing, existing.

The planet has done another spin around the sun, we’ve thrown colourful explosions into the sky, thousands of people have drunk themselves into an oncoming hangover, and the bottom right of my laptop now says 01:00, 01/01/24.
