2022-03-30 — heaven is a place on earth

i'm not afraid anymore

I found a new Doctor Who blog earlier, which deconstructs and analyses episodes piece by piece. The focus is on Chibnall’s era (error?) and I skim-read a bunch of posts. I’ve now read far too much analysis of what is essentially just a silly kids’ teatime adventure show, but I feel somewhat reassured that I’m not just having a breakdown on my own – there are other people out there seeing the same shit I’m seeing on TV every time “written by Chris Chibnall” shows up in giant gold font.
