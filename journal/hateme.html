i hate me

Just letting emotion pour out of me. I never feel like I can fully open up to anyone. I’ve been hurt too many times to trust anyone. And no matter what I do, who I talk to, I just end up back at the bottom of the pit. Climbing out every time is exhausting.

I hate that I feel such intense emotions. I swing from one extreme to the other and it’s knackering. I wish I didn’t. I wish I could just shrug it all off and carry on. I thought I’d got my emotions under control lately – sometimes I can, but eventually they just consume me, and my brain doesn’t leave me in peace.

I’m struggling so much atm. I’m so alone. I hate being me.
