night sky

2:30am. The night sky is so clear. An abundance of stars littering the sky. So pretty. We all get so wrapped up in day to day life that we forget just how beautiful this planet and this universe is.

The world asleep, so quiet, so calm.

2:30am, 24 hours later. Fewer stars in the sky tonight, but those visible are as bright as ever. The same winter chill on my chest. Snow dusting the tops of houses behind us, and absolute silence tonight. You could hear a pin drop. Peace. Momentary bliss.

I should enjoy the night sky more often. It does wonders for the soul.
