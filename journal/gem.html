gem

Gemma is fucking great. I adore her so much, and I’ve really got to know her properly this last year or so. I love when we get to spend time together – we’re both quite similar people, both riddled with anxiety, both suffering from bouts of depression, and we just gel so well. There’s no having to put on a mask or a brave face when we’re alone together, unlike when everyone else is around. I just appreciate her so fucking much, and I’m so glad she’s a part of my life.
