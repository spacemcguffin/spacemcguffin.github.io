incoherence

I’ve lost myself in amongst the grey.

The world at large feels increasingly more volatile and hostile. Everything is an extreme, and any sense of middle ground is being swept away. Humans are exhausting. I’m feeling stuck and stagnant, but I don’t even have the energy to even think of a way out right now. I’m just a passenger, on the outside looking in, and nothing seems to matter or mean anything. I don’t feel good enough, or able, or passionate. I’m just a hollow shell.

Forgive the stream of consciousness. I don’t really know what my point is, or if I even have one. I’m just flexing my fingers on the keyboard, and trying to purge my brain of a few mismatched thoughts. No point trying to apply any kind of logic to it. I just don’t know how to coherently explain anything anymore, nor how to find the will to give a damn. I need a release of some kind.
