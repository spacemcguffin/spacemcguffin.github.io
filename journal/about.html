about.

i made this site to help me heal from the things in my head. to say the words and set them free. i suppose it’s easier to deal with stuff when it’s staring me right in the face.

some of the things i say are upsetting. some are unproductive, some make no sense. this is not meant to be curated or comprehensible. this is me processing the world in real time. it’s unpretty, it’s immature, it’s downright ridiculous.

the background images are all mine, usually taken around the same time the entry was written. pictures were taken on my paper shoot camera, an old red canon powershot sd780, or a ricoh griiix. i take as much pride in my images as i do my words.
