06.01.23 — devoid of feeling

tldr; shit sucks but I’ll be fine.

I feel numb and distant. Like I don’t feel anything. I don’t care about stuff. TV shows or politics or sports or work or movies or the world, or just anything. I have no attention span. I can’t find any strength to give a damn. There are maybe 3 people I genuinely care about who make me fucking feel something. I just wanna go home, listen to music, and disappear, cos at least then I don’t have anything to worry or care about. I don’t have to project and be someone I’m not.

I feel like I have no sense of purpose. I don’t know what I want to do, or where I wanna be. Cos really, I don’t wanna be here. I just don’t wanna be stuck here where I never asked to be or want to be.

I’m tired of losing everyone that I get close to. Losing family and friends all the time. Or I’ll push people away because it’s easier and I’m toxic so it’s probably better for them anyway. Someone else won’t make me feel better but I just feel lonely and unloved, and all I want more than anything is to be held or kissed sometimes and feel alive, feel fucking human. I dunno. I feel like there’s something wrong with me and it’s like the whole world has someone else, and I never can.

I don’t understand what the point to anything is. I don’t have anyone that relies on me so I’m just a lonely wandering spirit, coming into work and living on autopilot, just trying to survive, trying to be positive for other people. Desperately trying just to be okay, and I’m tired of feeling lost, feeling like I’m sinking all the time and I just don’t know what to do.

Winter is always the worst. I need summer and sun and warmth. I got a love heart sweet this morning that said ‘too much’ – I’m definitely that.
