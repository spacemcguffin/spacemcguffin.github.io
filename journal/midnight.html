midnight soul

Lying in bed and the crescent moon is right in my line of sight. There are so many stars out tonight. I just had a couple of minutes at my window, feeling the gentle breeze against my chest and my face. It’s so peaceful, and so calm.

I cried at my window. Still a few tears now. I don’t know why. It’s just my body getting its release. I’m overwhelmed but I’m also okay, I think. Mixed emotions about things.

I can’t see the stars from my bed, but I can see the moon. It’s so bright. I wish I could share this with someone. It breaks my heart that I can’t.
