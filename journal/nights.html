nights like these

Had two nights of fireworks – both displays were visible from my garden, both pretty spectacular. There’s something childlike and wondrous about being outdoors on a frosty November night, watching fireworks. I wish I could’ve shared it with someone.

Gemma messaged me earlier to check in on me, which was lovely. People don’t usually do that. I appreciate her so much, and it was just nice to not be the one to start a conversation for a change. I’m so used to my own company, and I find I’m usually the one starting a conversation with people, so it genuinely made my evening when she just popped up to see how I was.
