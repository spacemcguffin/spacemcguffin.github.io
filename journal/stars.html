stars

1:30am. The last couple of nights have been cloudy, without stars. Tonight though, so many stars. The clouds parted to gift me clearest night sky. So many stars. It’s like the universe knew what I needed.

However crappy life on this planet is, whatever we mess up, there’s one thing humans can’t fuck up – the universe. We’ll always have a sky to look up at, and a magic to revel in.

Gazing up at the stars somehow makes it all okay. It’s all mine. It’s so peaceful. I love it.
