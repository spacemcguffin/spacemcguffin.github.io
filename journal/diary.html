I used to write an online diary. I wrote 2137 entries between 2008 and 2012, spanning my latter school years and time at college. I only told two of my friends about it. It was filled with drama, bitching, angst and heartbreak.

In 2021, I started writing again. This time, an offline diary. Recently, I wrote my 300th entry. Writing keeps me focused and grounded. I tend to write at the end of the day, sometimes in the dead of night. Unedited, unfiltered, unapologetic. I'd recommend it to anyone. Journal, diary, notes, whatever.

If you've been in my life, you've probably been written about.
