11.02.21 — momentary bliss

2:30am. Fewer stars in the sky tonight, but those visible are as bright as ever. The same winter chill on my chest. Snow dusting the tops of houses behind us.

Absolute silence and peace. Momentary bliss. Being a night owl isn’t always a bad thing.

There’s something quite fairytale about snow-dusted rooftops against a starry sky. It’s nice to just let go and feel like a kid again. A world full of wonder and magic. I should enjoy the night sky more often. It does wonders for the soul.
