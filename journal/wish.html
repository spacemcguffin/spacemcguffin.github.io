06.03.23 — wish upon a star

00:01am. I want to lie out under the stars and hold her hand. Stargazing with my soulmate. I want to feel the winter chill against my skin; the freshness on my face. I want to watch the night sky blacken as the clouds fade, revealing its starlit canvas beneath. I want to tilt my head to see her lying there beside me, pointing out her favourite stars.

Two heartbeats while the world sleeps. The earth beneath us, grounding us. Two kindred spirits in the dead of night.

I wish upon a star.
