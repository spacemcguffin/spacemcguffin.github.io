june 1st

Someone asked me recently if I had good memories of my dad. The truth is I only really remember his last few months or so. Hooked up to machines, hospices and hospital beds. I can’t even hear his voice anymore. It fucking kills me. Cancer fucking sucks.

Today has been a day. I realised half way through my shift that it was Thursday, and it everything just made sense. Of course it’s Thursday – me and Thursdays have never got on. I did see Zoe briefly, so I had a small moment of feeling free and okay with her. God love her.
