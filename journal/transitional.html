The clocks went back recently so it’s getting darker earlier. The days are becoming increasingly shorter. There’s more rain and grey sky than ever. It’s cosy indoors, but the transition into winter is nudging at my depression and draining me more than usual. It’s also the time of the year where I most crave hugs and someone to wrap my arms around and spend time with.

Another year, another year alone. Feeling isolated and left behind.
