16.03.21 — ephemerality

00:30am. I had the heaviest feeling in my stomach about an hour ago. Just an overwhelming sense of dread.

I wish we could take photographs with our eyes because I opened my window as wide as possible and all that anxiety just evaporated. The sky’s the clearest I’ve seen it so far this year. Completely awash with stars and patterns and beauty. I tried to take a photo with my phone but it was just black. Imagine if every time we blinked, if we chose, a snapshot was taken and then stored in our minds to be accessed anytime we wanted.

The sky tomorrow will be different, and there’s beauty in ephemerality and change, but I also wish I could look back on tonight’s sky.
