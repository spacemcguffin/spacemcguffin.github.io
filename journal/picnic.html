25.06.22 — picnic with a pal

Day one of my week off. I feel so chill and relaxed, and the weather has calmed down – it’s nowhere near as hot as it has been, but it’s still nice. I’ve been counting the days to this work break. It was nice to just lie in bed ‘til whenever my body needed to this morning.

I have had a nice week though, in amongst the chaos. A highlight has been getting to know one of our new visual merchandisers better. We’re the same age, and she’s so in tune with me and my sense of humour, and she’s really looked out for me this week. We’ve spent a couple of lunch times out on the grass behind work and I feel like I’ve made a new friend in her this last week. I just appreciate her a lot. ‘Tis nice.
