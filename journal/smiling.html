smiling through tears

Just perched on my windowsill ‘cos the sky is so clear tonight. There are so many stars. I’ve not done this in a while; just sat, calm, taking in the world, enjoying the quiet. I love the marvel of the universe. It’s funny how the stars, millions of miles away, keep me grounded. I find them so beautiful and wonderful. I feel lucky that I get to witness them.

I love the thought that we all get to share the same sky, the same stars, albeit from different vantage points all over the world. It’s comforting to think that someone I love might also be gazing out into the night sky, at the same stars, at the same time as me. It makes me feel connected to people, and it makes me feel like I belong.

The world is quiet. Everything seems peaceful. Smiling through tears.
