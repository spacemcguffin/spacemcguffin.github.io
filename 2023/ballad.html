the ballad of the first

January 12, 2023

00:20am

I discovered a new song tonight, This Is A Song For Miss Hedy Lamarr – by Jeff Beck and Johnny Depp. It’s beautiful and I’ve got it on loop.

January has been rough, and it’s only the twelfth day. My uncle is quickly deteriorating, which is just crap, and my emotions have been drifting all over the place. I confided in ████, albeit after work and via message, cos I didn’t wanna get upset again at work. I’ve also had to try and keep my feelings for ████ in check too.

It sucks that the world keeps taking people away, and it sucks that the people I feel a connection to are already with other people or just unattainable. As I said the other day, I’m just a wandering spirit, a lonely soul. Maybe I’m just meant to be.

My heart feels heavy.
