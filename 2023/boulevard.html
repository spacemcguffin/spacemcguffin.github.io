boulevard of broken hearts

February 4, 2023

Grateful again for Alice, Gemma, and Zoe again this week. Both of them are amazing, and their hugs are the best.

I’ve not seen much of Gemma at work lately so I got a lift home with her yesterday and we had a bit of a ramble. We’re on a late shift together on Tuesday so we’ll get more of a catch-up then, but it was nice to just chill with her after the crappiest week I’ve had in a while.

My uncle’s funeral was on Thursday. Strange day, but a nice service and good food afterwards. Had to awkwardly grin and bear a bit of ‘happy families’ at the gathering afterwards, but hey. I didn’t want to go back to work yesterday but I did, and I got through it, but I’m grateful for this weekend off now to just breathe and zone out. I’m trying not to keep hold of negative feelings, and I do feel like I’ve managed to let the last week go. Next week at work might be hard again, but I’ll go back on Monday with the intent of a fresh start and take each new day as it comes.

I should be seeing Amy soon too. It’ll be nice to see her for a proper catch-up.

I grabbed myself an URL this week too. I can’t believe lewis.lc wasn’t taken... so I took it.
