up in the air

February 22, 2023

Everything is up in the air this week. Work is just giving me so much anxiety at the minute because there’s a lot happening. The long and short of it is – I’ve applied for two new jobs, my current one could make me redundant at any time, and my current area manager is being a dick. Today is my one day off this week and he’s already ruined it by messaging me. I’m trying not to worry or overthink, but I can’t help being an anxiety-riddled mess.

There were also some crossed wires that caused a bit of upset between me and Gemma recently too, so I’ve been miffed off because of that. I spent a good couple of hours with her last night on the late shift – we were good, and stuff was sorted, but I hadn’t seen her much lately so it was nice to just spend some time together to properly catch up and clear the air. Alice also let me just sit quiet with her in her office for a while yesterday, and that helped me just calm down and breathe for a while. I bloody love the pair of them.

Being the anxious mess I am, I just hate when there’s no closure on stuff. I hate waiting around, worrying. I wanna sort stuff now. I wanna deal with stuff now. Hopefully I’ll be a lot more chill by the end of the week.
