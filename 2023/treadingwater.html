treading water

February 19, 2023

I hate the knot in my stomach I have because of work. I’m partly burying my head in the sand and treading water, cos it’s easier, but every day makes me anxious lately. I feel really burned out, and ground down. I feel like a shell of a person at the minute. I just hate change, and I don’t deal with it well. I’m full of worry and anxiety, and it sucks. It feels like I’m sinking again.

So, I’m gunna do the only thing I can right now. I’m gunna get an early night, sink into my bed, shut out the world, try and switch my brain off and just forget about everything for a while. Goodnight.
