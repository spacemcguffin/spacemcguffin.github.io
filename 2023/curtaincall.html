curtain call

March 13, 2023

I can’t remember the last time I wrote this early in the day. It’s only half 3, but I usually write my diary late at night.

I got home from work about an hour ago. Done, packed up, finished. It’s a strange day. I’d done everything I needed to by about 11 but I stuck around a bit to see a few people and lend a hand with some things. I saw Alice and gave her a hug and told her she’s amazing, and I spent a bit of time with Zoe and Gemma for the last proper time. It’s strange cos when I go back, in about two weeks, I’ll still be in the same building and the same workplace, just upstairs. Away from all my pals, even though I’ll still see them in passing and make time to have catch-ups.

I’ve been so used to just doing my own thing, being my own manager, and spending time having a good laugh with everyone. I’ve only just started to get to know Zoe, and I’ve only really become properly good friends with Gemma since last summer, so it sucks I’ve gotta move away. But hey. Still in the same building, just upstairs, with familiarity, and something new. And if it isn’t for me, I’ll just look for something else, and maybe even finally escape where I am altogether.

But for now, a couple of weeks to chill, reset, clear my head, and have a clean break. It’s been a long time coming.
