the light at the end

February 27, 2023

Feb 23rd – I had a breakdown at work and cried in front of managers. I found out unofficially that I’d be soon out of a job.

Feb 24th – It was officially confirmed I’d be made redundant. The company I work for is awful, and have handled the whole situation so badly. My area manager is a complete cunt. My anxiety was through the roof.

Did have a nice catch-up with Amy though, and we had tea together. I walked home from work that evening, in the dark. It was breezy but fair, and I definitely needed it. I just sauntered around, taking the scenic route, just trying to clear my head a bit.

Feb 25th – I spoke to floor manager Gemma at work after missing her all week due to meetings and days off. She told me not to fret and that there’d be a job for me. I won’t be in menswear anymore, I’ll be upstairs, but a job’s a job and the amount of relief I felt at knowing I had job security was immense. From rock bottom Friday to a ridiculously high Saturday.

Yesterday – I spent most of my shift talking to Zoe, and I was able to chill for the first time in a while. I could go into more about the whole work situation but it was crap enough living through it. I’m sort of over it all now. I just want my redundancy pay-out, my area manager to sod off, my company to do one (they won’t last long anyway, so good luck to ‘em), and to be rid of all this work anxiety. There’s light at the end of the tunnel now, and I’m a lot more chill.
