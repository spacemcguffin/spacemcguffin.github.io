state of meh
Date 24 August, 2022
Time 14:15

‘I wonder how this week will go.’
― monday morning, 22 August 2022

August 24

Monday was pretty good. It sorta fell off a cliff yesterday. Today is my day off, and I’m just zoned out, listening to music and eating chocolate. The thing is, I was in a crappy headspace all of last night and a little this morning, and I had so many thoughts, but I didn’t want to write about it. If I could somehow dump all my thoughts instantaneously somewhere, that’d be great, but I can’t. And I really didn’t have the energy nor the will to open up this diary and then splurge everything from the brain. Instead, it just sort of festered a bit til I numbed myself to it, and now I’m in this state of meh.

I’m not a cranky emo 14 year old anymore who writes an angsty diary, and the pace at which stuff changes makes it totally not worth bothering about. (Kinda why I don’t use Twitter either – you can vent or post an opinion, but then be over it minutes later, moved on, but that thought or whatever is just stuck there, and people can see and respond to it forever, long after it was relevant or had a meaning / purpose. But I digress.)

I also don’t want to use this as an outlet for bitching or petty drama. I don’t experience too much of that stuff now anyway – I’ve long outgrown it, and I try not to direct too much of my energy towards it. I’m pretty neutral and uncontroversial in my diary, often vague and non-specific, and I’m okay with that. It’s a much more peaceful diary than the one I kept in 2008 when my life was just drama because I was a teenager.

I realise the irony of writing about not wanting to write, and expressing my frustration in a way that doesn’t actually tackle or explain why I’m frustrated to begin with. But hey. Stuff passes, and I’m pretty adept to just numbing myself to shit and, maybe after a few days of moping, just getting the fuck on with stuff and putting everything back into perspective.
