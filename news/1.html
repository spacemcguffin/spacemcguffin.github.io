Diary entry

old and new

2:00am. This year’s London showcase was great, and Sam Ryder’s performance was joyous. One of the better showings of recent years. I sent out all my messages before the lines got busy. Remember those days. My favourite message came back from Lauren, who made my new year before it even began:

Lauren: Awww thanks Lewis! I’ve just been in the nursery faffing with all his new baby stuff and think I’m going to take the hat and socks from your little set as a going-home outfit! Happy New Year!

I’ve also had a few conversations over the course of the evening, with Guy, Liam, Amy, and cousin Gem. I realise two of those people I’ve never mentioned in my diary before. Guy has been an internet friend since about 2011, and Liam is one of those people you speak to once in a blue moon because you sort of know each other but not massively so.

There was also a post on the front page of Reddit yesterday that said: “I found my dad’s PlayStation 1 from when he was a kid.”

I am officially old.
