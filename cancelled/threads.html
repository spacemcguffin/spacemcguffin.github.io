Story

The Master tries to eliminate the Doctor by replacing his usual coat with a passively evil “Dupli-coat”. This coat transmats anything put into its pockets to the Master, subtly disarming the Doctor, and emits a low-level psychic field that will influence its wearer to pilot the Doctor’s TARDIS into a deadly trap.

After a wardrobe mix-up with his companion, the Doctor realises the true nature of the “Dupli-coat”. Managing to evade the trap, the Doctor sends a dangerous alien artifact through the coat's pocket that will 'deal with' the Master.
