The Monster Mash is the first episode of the Tiny Hat Doctor's series of adventures.

Story

The Tiny Hat Doctor arrives in a spooky mansion, wearing a specially themed tiny hat. He encounters Dracula and after discussing hit film series Hotel Transylvania, The Doctor establishes his catchphrase as "FINE."

The Doctor discovers that there is a Spirit haunting Dracula's Mansion and decides to stop it - heading to the party room. The Doctor does a speech to reason with the Spirit, who agrees to become the DJ for Dracula's party. They all play the song from the end of the first Hotel Transylvania film as the Doctor leaves.

Cast

    • Notes

  • The Tiny Hat Doctor originates from a joke tweet Jacob and Phoenix made on the PZP Twitter in January 2020. Naturally, Jacob later took this too far and made actual content with this character.
  • This episode was produced incredibly quickly, with it originating from a joke selfie of Phoenix wearing the tiny Halloween hat sent on Monday 26th, Jacob writing the script very early on the 27th, and the line recording and editing all being done on the 30th.
  • Despite being released first by over a year, this episode was written after the following four stories.
