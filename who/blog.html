18 May 2024

OK Boomer

Boom was a very mid-tier Moffat episode. It was a refreshing change of pace after the last couple of episodes, but still nothing spectacular or new. Seen it all before. Is this all Doctor Who is now?

11 May 2024

Bum Notes

I just finished watching Space Babies and The Devil’s Chord. What a weird couple of hours. Doctor Who is now full of tonal whiplash, breakneck speed, fantasy guff, winks to camera, more than ever before. I didn’t love it, but I didn’t hate it. This era certainly isn’t made for me, but it’s at least more competent than anything shat out between 2018 and 2022.
