Half the problem
Date 03 January, 2021
Featuring —

Four papers suddenly announced earlier that Jodie Whittaker is leaving Doctor Who at the end of Series 13. If so, that’s only half of the problem gone. Nothing changes if Chibnall doesn’t go. Le sigh. I need to watch some old Who to remind me of the show I used to love.

Had a nothingy day again. The news is exhausting, all fun is banned, the internet is noisy, and nobody gives a fuck about me. I’ve also eaten too many chocolate biscuits – again.<.p>
