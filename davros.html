Character

Davros is a mad scientist and the Evil Creator of the Daleks. He is one of the Doctor's most notorious and insane enemies, yet they do have great respect for each other. Davros has one arm with a rubber glove and lives inside a wheelie bin.

Adventures

Childhood

As a young boy, Davros was visited by Santa Claus, who hesitated to give Davros his LEGO set due to the evil acts Davros would later commit. However, Santa eventually decided to give Davros his present, both because he believed in redemption and also the Christmas Goblins would takeover if he did not. (The Day of the Davros)

Becoming Evil

Davros later became the Evil Creator of the Daleks. (Skaro’s Lament)

The Rise & Fall of Davros

Davros reigned over the Daleks as their leader, until the Daleks decided he couldn't be taken seriously and dropped him. (Skidmarks on Skaro)

Death

The Doctor met Davros for the final time and debated whether or not to kill him. In the end, Davros died after choking on mushroom soup. (Live and Let Davros)
