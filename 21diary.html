My 2021 diary is a spiritual successor to timelordwannabe, a LiveJournal diary I kept between 2008 and 2012.

timelordwannabe was created on 22nd February 2008, and my first diary entry was dated 27th February 2008. The last entry was on 9th March 2012. The diary contained 2137 entries, documenting my last two years of high school and my time at college.

10 years later...

My 2021 diary was created on 1st January 2021, and my first entry was written on the same day. This diary ended up containing 134 entries, bringing my overall total to 2271 diary entries. The month I wrote the least was April, with just 4 entries, and my biggest writing month was February, with 28 entries (the peak of lockdown).
