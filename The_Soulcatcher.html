The Soulcatcher

The Soulcatcher
Species Non-corporeal spirit
Place of origin Unknown
Appearance The Soulcatcher

"The feast of the souls will commence this night."
― The Soulcatcher

The Soulcatcher was a mysterious spirit that fed off negative emotions caused by death and grief. Its presence was easily determined by the existence of inexplicable fog that surrounded the building the Soulcatcher chose to possess. This fog was powerful enough to even jam and clog up TARDIS engines, causing them to malfunction.

List of Appearances

The Soulcatcher possessed Gladstone Cottage long before Gideon Gladstone had murdered his wife Edith in a fit of drunken rage. After Edith's death, the Soulcatcher trapped her spirit within the walls of the house and stuffed itself on the cycle of pain within the family. This caused Edith's spirit to haunt the cottage to some degree; with Edith's dying screams echoing throughout the walls of the house.

With the fog messing with the Fourteenth Doctor's TARDIS controls, she visited the cottage to uncover the truth behind what was causing it - where she found Gideon as a reclusive, alcoholic and grieving single father. Sympathising with him due to her recent loss of her companion Fleur, the Doctor's investigations discovered the truth behind Edith's death and what the Soulcatcher was doing within the cottage.

Having Gideon arrested and his children taken away from him, the Soulcatcher had nothing left to feed on - and so let Edith's spirit finally lay to rest. Where the Soulcatcher went next was unknown. (TV: The Soulcatcher)
