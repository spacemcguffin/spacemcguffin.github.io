the ghost of christmas patience, past and future or something

December 22

I did good. I gave Alice and Lauren their gifts today at work.

They opened them, Lauren cried a little, and Alice told me I did good. Given that I started the month having no clue what to get anyone for Christmas, everything turned out well in the end. I’m so glad Alice’s journal arrived in time. My Secret Santa recipient, Debbie, told me yesterday she was proper chuffed with her gift (obviously I didn’t let on that her Secret Santa was me), so that’s three out of three so far. Deborah took the day off sick, so I gave her gifts to her daughter to pass on, and tomorrow I’m taking Gemma’s little gift bag in, then I’m done.

I told Gemma today that I’d got her something. She doesn’t do gifts with people in work, so I pre-warned her that I had, and that I hadn’t done it for anything in return. She was so fucking cute about it and she means a lot. I haven’t really spoken to her much at work lately, just cos of shift patterns and being busy with jobs, so it was nice to have a few minutes together to have a ramble. She got a little teary cos her anxiety is all over the place, she had a little wobble, but we had a laugh and she just made my afternoon. She told me I was one of the people who made her feel comfortable and good at work, and I just wanted to hug her. Bless her heart.

Alice also dyed her hair a reddish ginge colour and it looks good on her. Still crushing on her, but hey. She’s a good pal. Today was just a good, wholesome day. Lots of joking about, Christmas vibes, presents, all the good menswear staff in, fun, chilled out. I wish more days were like today.

Since I got home, I’ve just stuffed my face with Malteser truffles and watched YouTube. Tomorrow’s my last shift before I’m off for Christmas Eve and Christmas Day. I’m working Boxing Day but I’ve got two days off that week, including New Year’s Eve. Work is quiet at the minute, and there’s a nice chilled out atmosphere, so I’m just enjoying it while it lasts.
