through the clouds

january 19th, 2024
9:03pm

January is a slow month, but I’m enjoying it. I feel like I’m in a good place; I feel balanced and okay. Tired, but that’s par for the course. A storm is due on Sunday, but at least the frost and snow should be gone. I’ve enjoyed the blue skies and the clear nights littered with stars. I’ll take more of those.

It’s been a calm start to 2024, and I feel like I’ve had a good work / life balance. I’ve left 2023 behind and started anew. Stuff that bothered me for the longest time, and lingered, is all gone.
