Will Gold (born 1996), better known online as Wilbur Soot, is an English YouTuber, Twitch streamer, and musician. He is best known for being the frontman and editor of the YouTube group channel SootHouse, creating the Editor Wilbur ARG while editing for YouTuber JackSucksAtLife, and writing the storyline of the popular Dream SMP server.

He has released an album on Spotify (Your City Gave Me Asthma), an EP (Maybe I Was Boring), and several viral comedy songs. Most recently, he released the three songs "I'm in Love With An E-Girl", "Internet Ruined Me", and "Your New Boyfriend", which form the e-girl song trilogy. Wilbur also joined Lovejoy, a British indie band which released its debut EP, Are You Alright?, on May 9, 2021.

Currently, he streams on Twitch and posts stream highlights on YouTube. He also occasionally uploads videos of other games and Minecraft 100 Player Challenges and appears in videos on TommyInnit's channel. He is well-known for his conversational streaming style and his acting in the Dream SMP lore.
