in words and play

february 22nd, 2021

Struggling to beat the blank white page today. Digitised some scribbles (I put literal scribbles of ideas into a Word document and tore pages out of my notebook). It feels good to physically tear pages out. It also feels good to do actual pen and paper scribbling in a notebook. But it also feels good typing them out into a document on my laptop. Maybe it gives me a false feeling of being productive, achieving something, even if they’re just gunna collect pixelated dust in a folder now instead of being buried in turned pages on the side of my desk.

I miss writing as way of escapism. I’m glad I’ve managed to get back into diary writing this year, even if it is a little haphazardly. It’s like I’ve rediscovered a secret den where I can go and hide out for a while and just not worry about anything. I’ll have to come out eventually, but it’s a comforting hideaway for a little while, and a place free from rules and structure and society.
