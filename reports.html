📋 June 1998. Reception. Class RD.

English. Lewis has made excellent progress with reading and clearly enjoys reading very much. He is able to build simple three-letter words and use picture clues to help with words he does not know. He writes very well for himself, attempting his own spellings and using a wordbook. His work is always neatly presented and he is trying hard to reduce the size of his writing. He concentrates well during discussions.

History. Lewis has been very interested in finding out about things that happened in the past. He can describe changes in his own life. He is able to put objects and events into the correct order and is able to state the days of the week.

📋 June 1999. Year 1. Class 1A.

IT. Lewis has continued to develop his word processing skills. He has used the computer to create a design.

Art. Lewis has developed a good attention to detail in his work. He enjoys using a variety of media.

📋 June 2000. Year 2. Class 2K.

Science. Lewis is always interested in finding out why things happen and how things work. He has good observation skills and has his own ideas about how to find answers to questions.

IT. Lewis has good word processing skills and can type, edit and print his own work. He is aware of the use of computers in school and in the outside world.

Design & Technology. Lewis has designed and made a lovely calendar, pop-up cards and models, selecting and choosing the appropriate tools, materials and techniques.

📋 June 2000. Headteacher's Comment.

A satisfactory year’s work, but he should try to be more responsible when he is not under direct supervision.
