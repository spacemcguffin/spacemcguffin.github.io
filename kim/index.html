A quieter editorial page for lewis.lc

April 5, 2026 • Inter + Onest

This is a stripped-back version of the Lewis.lc aesthetic — keeping the identity, but removing the heavier glow and layered effects.

Instead of neon dominating the page, it now sits quietly in the background — a faint purple and cyan wash that gives the page atmosphere without distraction.

Why this works

It feels calmer. More intentional. The typography leads, the layout supports, and the colour just gently holds everything together.

Not every page needs to glow to feel like Lewis.lc.

This is probably the version I’d use for longer writing — diary entries, retrospectives, or anything more reflective.
