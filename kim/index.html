Letter #2

For Kim.

It’s 2026 so everything gets a sequel. I wanted to write some more. Don’t worry – this isn’t a regular thing. But if the previous ramble was more on the soft and sensual side, maybe this one will be more explicit and sexual. I’ve even whacked a footnote or two in this one! Get my grade bumped up please, or else I’ll come see you after class so you can teach me what to improve on 😉

Right now, I’m writing this just to get everything out of my head. I don’t even know if I’m going to give this you. I want to, but will I?

K – Drunk me is feral. Severely feral.

So when are we going out for a drink? It’s pot luck with me – after a drink I’m either giddy, emosh, or the horniest I’ve ever been. Question is, do you want to take the chance? (Let’s be fair – if I’m drinking with you, we know what the most likely outcome is. I’m already the horniest I’ve ever been for you, and I’m admitting that whilst stone cold sober these days.)

Why this works

It feels calmer. More intentional. The typography leads, the layout supports, and the colour just gently holds everything together.

Not every page needs to glow to feel like Lewis.lc.

This is probably the version I’d use for longer writing — diary entries, retrospectives, or anything more reflective.

— Lewis
